About Me

Welcome to my personal page! Here’s a little more about me, my journey, and my aspirations.

Biography

My name is Linus Dolck, and I’ve always been fascinated by technology. One of my earliest memories is when I was 10 years old, playing games on an old computer with my dad. I remember the excitement of playing games and trying to understand how the computer worked.

Academic Interests & Hobbies

My interest in web development grew during high school when I started experimenting with JavaScript and creating small interactive projects. I love the creative process of designing and building something from scratch. Outside of coding, I enjoy cooking, photography, and exploring new technologies. These hobbies allow me to express my creativity in different ways.

Future Goals

One of my biggest goals is to work with AI and finance. I believe that artificial intelligence has the potential to revolutionize the financial industry by making it more efficient and accessible. I want to contribute to this transformation by developing innovative tools that help people make better financial decisions.
